Anonymous Payroll Report

All processing happens in the browser, so your data never leaves your computer.

1. Prep

A) Download payslips

What to download
  • From your email, download all payslips for the dates you want to review (ideally for your entire employment).
Note: Ask your manager if you need a password to open them, or if you are missing any dates.

B) NEST contributions

Logging into NEST
  • If you've never logged into NEST, first create your account here: Sign up for NEST
Note: Your NEST member number can be found on any letter they've sent - it looks like this:
MEM048559238
What to download

Nest provides a pension contributions record for each financial year.

After loggin in visit: NEST: Contribution history

  1. Select a Tax Year

    You will repeat this process for each financial year.

  2. Select current Employer

    Select employer (current company name, if more then one).

  3. Tick both contribution types

    Important: Choose both "From your salary" and "From your employer".

  4. Click 'Apply Filters' & 'Download'

    Click "Apply filters" and then the "Download Results" buttons.

Rinse and repeat - You will need change the year and click "apply filters" and "download results" for each financial year.

3. Next Steps

A) Email your manager

If you note any discrepencies in your pension contributions, or deductions firt email your manager with these concerns. You may wish to forward them a copy of this report as evidence.

B) Verify Tax Payments

If you have any concerns about your tax payments, you should verify them with HMRC - eg your payslip is missing deductions taxcode or National Insurance ID. You can do this by downloading the HMRC app from the Apple App or Google Play Stores.

C) Join Unite

If you're not already a member, why not join Unite?

Note: Unite can advise, but usually can't provide legal help for issues reported within four weeks of joining.
